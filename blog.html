Why?

Communication is the key to education and understanding, and there is no better way to communicate than through meaningful stories.

If it is true that there is no shortage of news related to cybersecurity, it is also true that most of them do not belong in the educational bucket. Many are driven by sensationalism, sounding more like marketing messages that thrive on fear, uncertainty, and doubt. There is not much to learn for those not deeply immersed in the cybersecurity industry, and few comprise very little educational matter relevant for business owners and managers.

With our blog, we wish our readers to learn more about the role of technology in our society and business, pairing that with the cyber threats that can potentially impact them. Ultimately, it's all about the overall value of cybersecurity.

Our team stays tuned and alert for what is happening worldwide, identifying the latest trends and outlining the latest solutions â€“ all, so you do not have to. And when we decide that a story is essential to share with you, we aim to do that understandably and educationally.

We hope you find our content valuable for you, your business, and your community.
